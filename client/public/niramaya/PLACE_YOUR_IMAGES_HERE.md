# Niramaya Wellness Image Assets

To make the **Niramaya** website images render correctly, search for high-quality, non-copyright images on Unsplash or any free stock photo site, name them exactly as shown below, and place them inside this folder (`client/public/niramaya/`):

## 1. Hero & Branding Graphics
*   `hero-bottle.png` (or `.jpg`): The main product bottle (e.g., Vitamin D3+K2) displayed in the center of the hero section.
*   `capsules-preview.jpg`: A close-up or circular thumbnail of health capsules/pills.
*   `about-herbs.jpg`: An image displaying raw herbs, powders, or wellness elements for the About page.

## 2. Product Catalog Images
*   `vit-d3.jpg`: Product image for Vegan Vitamin D3 + K2 bottle.
*   `ashwagandha.jpg`: Product image for Ashwagandha Gold KSM-66 bottle.
*   `collagen.jpg`: Product image for Marine Collagen Peptides bottle or pack.
*   `omega3.jpg`: Product image for Premium Omega-3 Fish Oil softgels or bottle.
*   `electrolyte.jpg`: Product image for Hydrate Electrolytes powder or bottle.
*   `curcumin.jpg`: Product image for Organic Turmeric Curcumin bottle or capsules.

## 3. Blog Cover Images
*   `blog-ashwagandha.jpg`: Cover photo for the Ashwagandha Cortisol study article (e.g., a smiling woman or herbal extract).
*   `blog-vitd3.jpg`: Cover photo for the Vitamin D3/K2 Calcium Synergy article (e.g., pill capsules or soft sunlight).
*   `blog-morning.jpg`: Cover photo for the Vedic Morning Rituals article (e.g., lemons, citrus flat lay, or wellness tea).
